What is Ceres Cart?

Ceres Cart helps you seamlessly shop for ingredients while browsing your favorite recipes. With one click, you can:

  • Add recipe ingredients directly to your shopping cart.
  • View recipes in a clean, distraction-free reader mode.
  • Save time and effort with intuitive grocery list integration.

Getting Started

  1. Navigate to a recipe page.
  2. Click on the Ceres Cart icon in your browser toolbar.
  3. Select the ingredients you need and add them to your cart.
  4. Use the reader view for an uncluttered recipe experience.

It's that simple!
